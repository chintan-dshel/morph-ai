Material density evolves over iterations

Start: uniform grey slab. Each iteration removes under-stressed elements and reinforces load paths. After ~60 iterations: crisp solid/void topology.

03 — Physics Intuition

Fixed face · Load face · Stress paths

Material concentrates where it carries stress. Everything else is removed — like a sculptor revealing what was already there.

σ_phys = σ_norm × F [N] / (dx [mm] × t [mm])

04 — The Core Concept

Minimum material — maximum stiffness

The optimizer solves: given a design space, material, and loads — what is the least material needed to not fail?

It minimizes compliance (= maximizes stiffness) subject to a volume fraction limit. The result is structurally identical to hand-engineered parts — achieved automatically.
